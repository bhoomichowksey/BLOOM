## 🩺 Problem Statement

1 in 10 women suffer from PCOS. Millions more face endometriosis, irregular cycles, and mental health challenges — yet most digital health tools are designed for men. Women lack access to a safe, intelligent, and personalized health companion that understands their unique needs.

---

## 💡 Solution — Bloom

**Bloom** is an AI-powered women's wellness app that provides:
- 🔴 **Cycle Tracker** — Log period dates, flow intensity, symptoms, and notes
- 😊 **Mood Tracker** — Track mood (1–5 scale), energy levels, and journal thoughts
- 🤖 **AI Wellness Chat** — Powered by Google Gemini; empathetic, evidence-based health guidance 24/7
- 🔐 **Secure Auth** — Full user authentication and personal health data privacy
